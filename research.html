Research

Urban climate, exposure, and environmental equity.

01

Research Interests

My research focuses on urban environmental change under climate stress, with particular attention to urban shade, heat exposure, green-space accessibility, land-use change, habitat quality, environmental equity, and adaptation planning.

02

Current Questions

How well do urban shadows align with radiative demand across global cities? How does rapid urbanization alter the probability of green-space exposure? Which spatial configurations of vegetation, buildings, and land use produce more equitable and climate-resilient urban environments?

03

Emerging Directions

I am developing work that links three-dimensional urban form, satellite remote sensing, clean-energy infrastructure, and climate-health evidence. Recent projects also explore large language models for environmental text analysis and evidence synthesis.

04

Methods & Approaches

My methodological toolkit includes Google Earth Engine, Python, R, spatial econometric models, GeoAI, urban remote sensing, street-view imagery, spatial syntax, InVEST, Linkage Mapper, Fragstats, Conefor, field verification, and policy-oriented evidence synthesis.
