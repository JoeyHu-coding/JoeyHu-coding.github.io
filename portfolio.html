01 - Featured Work - Course Studio

Shanshui Stitching

山水缝合 - Xuanwu Lake-Zijin Mountain Connection Belt Planning and Design

The project explores how a fragmented urban interface between Xuanwu Lake and Zijin Mountain can be repaired through ecological corridors, public-space stitching, pedestrian experience design, and landscape infrastructure.

Time
Mar 2023 - May 2023
Site
Xuanwu Lake-Zijin Mountain area, Nanjing
Scale
29 ha
Role
Group work; responsible for master planning, GIS analysis, overall design coordination, rendering, and subarea design.

02 - Urban Wetland Renewal - Course Studio

Blue-Green Reconstruction

蓝绿重构 - Urban Wetland Park Renewal Based on Environmental Experience and Nature Education

This studio work focuses on wetland renewal in a dense urban district, using blue-green structure, environmental experience, and nature-education programming to reshape ecological accessibility and public engagement.

Time
Sep 2022 - Nov 2022
Site
Yuhuatai District, Nanjing
Scale
120 ha
Role
Group work; responsible for early-stage analysis and master plan development.

03 - Individual Design - Campus Architecture

Wings of Yanji

燕几之翼 - Campus Leisure Station Design

An individual design project for a compact campus leisure station, combining landscape setting, architectural form, circulation, lightweight structure, and interior-exterior experience.

Time
Mar 2022 - May 2022
Site
Huai'an Campus, Nanjing Forestry University
Scale
300 m2
Role
Individual work; responsible for concept, modeling, drawings, renderings, and presentation boards.
