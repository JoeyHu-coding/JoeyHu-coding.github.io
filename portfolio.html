01 - Featured Work - Course Studio

Shanshui Stitching

山水缝合 - Xuanwu Lake-Zijin Mountain Connection Belt Planning and Design

The project repairs a fragmented interface between Xuanwu Lake and Zijin Mountain through ecological corridors, public-space stitching, pedestrian experience design, and landscape infrastructure.

Time
Mar 2023 - May 2023
Site
Xuanwu Lake-Zijin Mountain area, Nanjing
Scale
29 ha
Role
Group work; responsible for master planning, GIS analysis, overall design coordination, rendering, and subarea design.
