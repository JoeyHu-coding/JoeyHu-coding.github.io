Academic Service

Reviewer for urban climate, ecology, and environmental journals.

Awards

Selected scholarships, academic honors, and competitions.

Selected honors include the 2025 Nitori International Scholarship at Fudan University, 2021 National Scholarship, 2024 Jiangsu Province Outstanding Undergraduate Thesis, 2024 Outstanding Graduate, 2022 Advanced Individual in Scientific and Technological Innovation, and Merit Student honors in 2021, 2022, and 2023.

Writing & Reports

Books, reports, briefs, and research communication.

I contributed to Nature-Based Solutions: Principles and Applications from Fudan University Press, mainly supporting Chapter 2, "Nature-Based Solutions for Multiple Challenges." I also contribute to the 2025 China report of the Lancet Countdown on health and climate change and related international climate-health observation briefs.

Workshops / Visits

International and interdisciplinary training experiences.

Additional academic experiences include visiting study at Osaka University's Yabuki Lab from December 2021 to March 2022, participation in DigitalFUTURES 2022 "One Planet" summer workshop, and research activity with the Digital City and Human Settlements Laboratory at Nanchang University from 2022 to 2023.

Leadership

Service roles in academic and student communities.

At Fudan University, I have served as a committee member of the eighth master's student party branch in the Department of Environmental Science and Engineering and as a member of the Academic Department of the graduate student union. At Nanjing Forestry University, I served as class monitor and as a staff member of the university Youth League Committee.

Science Communication

Urban Ecology and Geography Insight.

I independently operate UEG-Insight, a research feed focused on urban geography, big data, human-environment analysis, resource and energy sustainability, and Earth system science. The work involves screening frontier journal articles and communicating research trends to a wider academic audience.
